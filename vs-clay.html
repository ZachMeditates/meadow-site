What makes Meadow different

For friends, not contacts

Clay treats people as data points in a professional network. Meadow treats them as friends you want to stay close with. Different tools for different jobs.

Simple by design

Clay has 50+ integrations and a learning curve that takes time to master. Meadow has one input: text a message. Everything else happens automatically.

Proactive, not passive

Clay enriches profiles and waits for you to act. Meadow sends you a brief every Monday with who to call, what to talk about, and a ready-to-send message. It nudges you daily when close friends are slipping away.

Costs less, does more (for friendships)

$8/mo vs $20/mo. If you are tracking friends and family, not investors and business leads, Meadow does more with less. Self-host for free if you prefer.

Where Clay still wins

We believe in honest comparisons. Here's where Clay has the edge.

Data enrichment

Clay automatically pulls news, job changes, and social media updates from dozens of sources. Meadow only knows what you tell it.

Integration depth

Clay connects to 50+ tools for automatic contact creation and management. Meadow works through Telegram with a web dashboard.

Professional networking

Clay is purpose-built for power networkers and business development. If your goal is to manage investors, clients, or sales leads, Clay is the better tool.

Team features

Clay offers shared workspaces for team collaboration. Meadow is built for individual use.
