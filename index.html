Privacy & Security

Your data, your rules

Isolated storage

Every account gets its own database. Your data physically cannot mix with anyone else's.

No training, no selling, no sharing

Your data is never used to train models, sold to advertisers, or shared with third parties.

Hardened by default

Encrypted fields, secure sessions, bcrypt passwords, and security headers on every response.

Export everything, anytime

Download all your data as JSON or CSV. If you leave, it leaves with you. No lock in.

FAQ

Common questions

Juniper Friends is a personal CRM that lives in Telegram. You text it naturally about the people in your life, and it remembers everything: birthdays, jobs, life updates, how you met. It nudges you when someone is overdue for a check in and sends you a weekly brief with who to reach out to and what to talk about.

Open Telegram and message Juniper like you would a friend. Say things like "had coffee with Sarah, she got promoted" or "remind me to call Mike next week." Juniper parses the details, stores them, and uses them to generate personalized nudges and weekly briefs. There is also a web dashboard where you can browse contacts, view interaction timelines, and explore a visual relationship map.

Yes. Every account gets its own isolated database. Your data is never used to train AI models, never sold to advertisers, and never shared with third parties. Sensitive fields like phone numbers and emails are encrypted at rest. You can export or delete all your data at any time.
