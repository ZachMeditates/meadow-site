Features

Everything you need to
be the friend who remembers

Natural Language Logging

Just text like you'd text a friend. "Had coffee with Sarah, she's thinking about moving to Austin." Juniper Friends extracts the who, what, when, and life updates automatically.

Smart Reminders

"Remind me to check in with Mike next week." Set it and forget it. Juniper Friends will surface it when it matters. Which your brain was definitely going to do on its own. Definitely.

Relationship Tiers

Organize your people into inner circle, close friends, and orbit. Juniper Friends tracks contact frequency and flags when someone's overdue. Think of it as a friendship smoke detector.

Semantic Search

"Who was thinking about changing jobs?" Search by meaning, not keywords. Juniper Friends understands context and finds the right people even from fuzzy queries.

Web Dashboard

A visual command center for your social life. Browse contacts, view interaction timelines, copy draft messages, and explore your relationship map.

Full Undo Support

Logged the wrong thing? Merged the wrong contacts? Every action is reversible. Just say "undo" and Juniper Friends rolls it back, no questions asked.

Export Anytime

Your data belongs to you. Export everything as JSON or CSV whenever you want. Import contacts from a spreadsheet just as easily. No lock-in, no hostage situations.

Privacy First

Your contact data is never sold and never used to train anything. Every account is sandboxed in its own database. We built this for trust, not ads.

Birthday Tracking

Never be the friend who texts "happy belated" again. Juniper Friends flags upcoming birthdays in your weekly brief and gives you time to reach out before the day passes.

Security

The boring but
important stuff

You're trusting Juniper Friends with the names, birthdays, and life details of the people you care about. That trust comes with responsibility. Here's how we handle it.

Isolated Storage

Every account gets its own database, completely separate from every other user. There is no shared table, no shared query, no way for data to cross over.

Hardened by Default

Security headers on every response. Clickjacking protection, content injection prevention, and strict referrer policies baked in from day one.

No Data Sharing

Your contact data is never sold, never shared, never used to train models. It exists for one purpose: helping you be a better friend. Period.

Secure Sessions

Passwords hashed with bcrypt. Sessions stored in secure, httponly cookies that can't be read by scripts. Rate limiting on authentication to block brute force attacks.
