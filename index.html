Features

Everything you need to
never drop the ball again

Natural Language Logging

Just text like you'd text a friend. "Had coffee with Sarah, she's thinking about moving to Austin." Juniper extracts the who, what, when, and life updates automatically.

Smart Reminders

"Remind me to check in with Mike next week." Set it and forget it. Juniper will surface it when it matters, in your brief and on your dashboard.

Relationship Tiers

Organize your people into inner circle, close friends, and orbit. Juniper tracks contact frequency and flags when someone's overdue for a check-in.

Semantic Search

"Who was thinking about changing jobs?" Search by meaning, not keywords. Juniper understands context and finds the right people even from fuzzy queries.

Web Dashboard

A visual command center for your social life. Browse contacts, view interaction timelines, copy draft messages, and explore your relationship map.

Full Undo Support

Logged the wrong thing? Merged the wrong contacts? Every action is reversible. Just say "undo" and Juniper rolls it back, no questions asked.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

01

Text Juniper

Open Telegram and talk to Juniper like a friend. Add people, log hangouts, set reminders. No forms, no menus, just conversation.

02

Juniper Remembers

Every detail gets parsed, organized, and stored. Life updates, birthdays, jobs, interests. Juniper builds a living profile of every person in your world.

03

Stay Connected

Get a weekly brief every Monday with who to reach out to, ready-to-send messages, upcoming birthdays, and reminders. Copy, paste, send.
