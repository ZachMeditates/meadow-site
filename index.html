What is Meadow?

Meadow is a personal CRM that runs inside Telegram. You text it about your friends in plain language, and it automatically tracks contacts, logs interactions, and builds relationship profiles. Every Monday, it sends a weekly brief with who to reach out to, what to talk about, and a ready-to-send draft message. Hosted plans start at $8/month. Self-hosting with Docker is free. Learn more about what a personal CRM is, see how Meadow compares to other personal CRMs, or read about using Meadow for networking, parenting, or staying in touch with friends. A personal CRM (customer relationship manager) is a tool for keeping track of the people in your life. Unlike business CRMs built for sales teams, a personal CRM helps you remember birthdays, life updates, and when you last talked to someone. Meadow is a personal CRM designed specifically for friendships.

Most personal CRM apps require you to open a web app and fill out forms. Meadow is conversational: you text it naturally, like talking to a friend, and it extracts the details automatically. It lives in Telegram so there is no new app to install. And instead of just storing data, Meadow notices when you're losing touch with someone and writes the first message for you.
